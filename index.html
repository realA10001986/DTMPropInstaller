Welcome to the CS/A10001986 DTM Prop Installer

This page is intended to ease firmware installation on fresh NodeMCU ESP32 boards for use in CircuitSetup/A10001986-designed DTM props such as Time Circuits Display, Flux Capacitor, SID, Dash Gauges or VSR.

Using this page, you can install a generic OTA Installer on your ESP32. This generic Installer allows OTA installation of the respective prop's firmware on your ESP32.

Note that you might need to install some driver before proceeding:

  • For ESP32 boards with a CP210x, install this. On Windows, connect your ESP32, start the Device Manager, expand the "Ports (COM & LPT)" list and look for the port with the ESP32 name. On Mac, the port is named "/dev/cu.SLAB_USBtoUART".
  • For ESP32 boards with a CH340, Windows users need to install a driver as well. Mac users don't. On Windows, see above on how to find out about the port; on Mac, the port is named /dev/cu.usbserial-XXXX (XXXX being some random number).

In order to proceed, connect your NodeMCU ESP32 to your computer through USB, wait a few seconds, and click "Connect".

After installation, disconnect the ESP32 from your computer, install it in/on your prop, and power-up the prop. Next, connect your computer or hand-held to a WiFi network named "NP-AP". Then navigate your browser to http://install.local or http://192.168.4.1 and click on "Update". On the page presented then, select a firmware binary for either TCD, FC, SID, Dash Gauges or VSR to install, and click "UPDATE" to proceed. The firmware will be installed on your ESP32. Future firmware updates can be done OTA through the prop's firmware, a re-installation of the generic Installer - as presented on this page - is not required.
